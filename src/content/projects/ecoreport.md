## Overview

EcoReport is a SaaS platform that helps companies produce sustainability reports
aligned with the TSRS / ESRS frameworks. The core idea is to take the heavy,
manual reporting workflow and accelerate it with LLM-powered drafting, so teams
spend less time wrestling with disclosures and more time on the substance. It's
being incubated at Erciyes Teknopark's Sera program.
